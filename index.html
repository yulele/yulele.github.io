Dr. Yu Le

Dr. Yu is a teacher of Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications a. In 2021, he received his Ph.D. degree from the same university under the supervisor Dr. Xiapu Luo. After that, he became a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Computing, Hong Kong Polytechnic University (Aug 2021-June 2023). Dr.Yu focuses on combining program analysis and natural language processing (NLP) techniques to solve the security and privacy issues contained in Android apps, IoT devices, and vehicles (Google Scholar Homepage).

Currently, Dr. Yu is looking for highly motivated PhD students and research assistants in the areas of Mobile/IoT/System Security and Privacy, Software Engineering. If you have interests, please send emails to yulele08 [at] gmail.com.
